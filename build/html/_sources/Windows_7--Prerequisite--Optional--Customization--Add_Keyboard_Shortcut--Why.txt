Why do you have to do it
========================

Have you ever experienced the feeling that you have lost a good idea because your mouse moves and your double clicks to open folders and programs took you too many time...? 

Definitely yes ! And you know why ? 

Because you don't have `keyboard shortcuts`_ to your favorite programs !

Nothing goes faster than a `keyboard shortcuts`_ except the light and... your ideas.
